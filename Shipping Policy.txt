Shipping & Store Pickup Policy

ஸ்ரீ கிருஷ்ணா பர்னிச்சர்ஸ் (Shree Krishna Furnitures), Mambakkam


We operate as a dedicated micro-business specializing in high-quality, custom-made wooden furniture. To provide the best value without the overhead of shipping logistics, we exclusively support in-store factory pickup.

1. No Shipping Services Provided

Please be advised that Shree Krishna Furnitures does not arrange or cover the cost of shipping or home delivery.

2. Made-to-Order Timelines

All items listed in our catalog are made-to-order based on customer specifications.

Production Time: Orders are typically ready for collection within 3 to 5 weeks from the date of order confirmation. We will notify you immediately once your item is complete.

3. The Pickup Process

Location: Pickup is required at our factory shop location:

Address: No.1/182, Kolathur Main Road (Opposite to Mambakkam EB Office),

Mambakkam, Vandalur Taluk, Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600127.

Transport Responsibility: The customer assumes full responsibility for arranging appropriate transportation (vehicle size, padding, tie-downs) and sufficient manual labor for loading the finished product from our factory premises.

4. Professional On-Site Assembly

We ensure the furniture is correctly assembled for maximum durability. We offer professional fitting/assembly services at your home after you have transported the components to your location.
